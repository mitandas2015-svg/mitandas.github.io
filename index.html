About Me

With a strong interest in applying Geospatial technologies in the field of water resources, my research work centres on assessing and identifying microplastic contamination in soils in urban catchment using advanced geospatial techniques. By integrating Remote sensing datasets and Geospatial technologies, I aim to identify potential microplastic contamination zones.
