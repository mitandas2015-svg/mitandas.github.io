Research / Academic Projects

Assessment of Geomorphic Indices in the Nagaland and Manipur regions of the Indo-Burma Wedge (M.TECH.)

Duration: 2023-2024 | Role: Student

Comprehensive Assessment of Relative Tectonic Activity using geomorphic indices, lineaments, and multi-criteria decision-making processes. Integrated remote sensing data with field observations to evaluate relative tectonic activity potential in the region.

Key Outcomes: Developed New methodology and Part of the research has been presented in an International Conference.

Assessment of Population Density Along Indian Rivers (B.TECH.)

Duration: 2017-2018 | Role: Student

Large-scale population density mapping using GPS technology and census data across major Indian river basins. Analyzed distribution of populations on various parts of indian rivers.

Tools Used: GPS, Census Data

Small Township Project in Kunda Village, Jharkhand, India (DIPLOMA)

Duration: 2015 | Role: Student

Comprehensive township planning including road alignment, drainage design, and infrastructure surveying using modern geospatial technologies (leveling, total stations, GPS) integrated with census data for demographic analysis.

Skills Applied: Land surveying
